Welcome to AIUTO


AIUTO is the most humble product we meet and live with every day. It feels that it's meant to be. Silly childhood game of selling bags made out of handkerchief suddenly started looking like a signal. Su and Ta always had a special liking for the countless and varied forms of art and culture across India. Even after spending many years in the corporate world, theirpassion never abated. Thus in 2016, fighting many odds, AIUTO was born.


The brand AIUTO is a fresh design house that centers on creating a range of attire with a blend of tradition and contemporaneity. aiuto’s crafted inspirations pay attention to details and the designs add elegance, boldness and grace. AIUTO or thread, the basis of the brand's inspiration is a reminder of the craft traditions of India. Like a pure creation that begins with a needle and thread, AIUTO offers novelty each time it is experienced.


AIUTO is the maker of simple and humble designs using our very own and known fabrics to make our souls sing with joy. Artisans from all around the country create raw and uninhibited designs usingtheir ancestral weaving processes. AIUTO talks to tradition and weaves its own creation. We salute their craftsmanship and are thankful to them for keeping this art alive. AIUTO products are just a token of representation of the innumerous souls who have touched the fabric. We aspire to keep the undying spirit of artistry alive.
